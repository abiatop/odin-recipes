Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 (14.5-ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 pound spaghetti

Instructions:

  1. In a large saucepan, cook the ground beef over medium heat until it is browned, about 5-7 minutes. Add the onion, carrot, celery, and garlic, and cook for an additional 5 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  2. Stir in the diced tomatoes, beef broth, oregano, basil, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using). Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook according to package directions until al dente. Drain the spaghetti and return it to the pot.
  4. Add the bolognese sauce to the spaghetti and toss to combine. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
