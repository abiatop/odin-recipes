LasagnaParma

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (6-ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 8 lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large saucepan, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until meat is browned and onion is tender. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water, parsley, salt, pepper, basil, and oregano. Reduce heat to low, and simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions. Drain, and rinse with cold water.
  4. In a small bowl, beat together ricotta cheese and egg.
  5. Spread 1/2 cup meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange 4 noodles over sauce. Drop spoonfuls of ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles. Sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese. Top with 1/2 cup meat sauce, and 1/4 cup mozzarella cheese.
  6. Repeat layers, and top with remaining noodles, meat sauce, Parmesan cheese, and mozzarella cheese.
  7. Cover dish with aluminum foil.
  8. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
