Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1/4 cup sliced olives
  • 1/4 cup sliced pepperoni (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to the desired shape and thickness. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza pan.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border around the edge. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  4. Add the mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, and pepperoni (if using). Sprinkle the Parmesan cheese over the top.
  5. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
